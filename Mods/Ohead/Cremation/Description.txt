During the endgame, people start dying faster than they can be buried.
Cremation is the solution! It's a mode for firekeepers, unlocked from ironworking.

-----

How to install the mod?

To install the mod, you need to paste the mod link into the mod box.

1. Click the blue button on the top right that says in white lettering "Use Mods".
2. Under the line of code saying "data.js" put:
https://therealohead.github.io/neverending-legacy-mods/mods/cremation.js
