Firepit crafting:
Fire Pits can now be crafted using coal. A new mode is available for the [firekeeper] called "Coal fire" that will convert 5 sticks and 5 coal into 1 Fire Pit.

firekeeper:
https://legacygame.fandom.com/wiki/Firekeeper

Filtering water:
Artisans have access to a new mode called "Water filter" that will convert muddy water using sand and coal.

How to install the mod?

To install The Coal Mod, you need to paste the mod link into the mod box.

1. Click the blue button on the top right that says in white lettering "Use Mods".
2. Under the line of code saying "data.js" put:
https://brunosupremo.github.io/coal-mod/coal_mod.js
